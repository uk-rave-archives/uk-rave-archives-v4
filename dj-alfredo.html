DJ Alfredo

The selector at the heart of the Ibiza story. His open-minded sound at Amnesia helped inspire the Balearic Beat and the British acid house explosion.

Biography

The sound of Amnesia

DJ Alfredo became one of the defining figures of Ibiza's 1980s club culture. His sets at Amnesia were known for moving freely between styles rather than staying in one lane.

That eclectic approach — house, pop, rock, soul, electronic records and atmosphere — helped create what later became known as the Balearic sound.

For a group of British DJs visiting Ibiza in the late 1980s, the experience became a turning point. The feeling they heard and felt on the island fed directly into the early UK acid house movement.
